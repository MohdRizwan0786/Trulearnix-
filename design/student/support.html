Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my certificate?
Go to My Certificates section and click Download PDF on any completed course certificate.
When will my withdrawal be processed?
Withdrawals are processed within 3-5 business days after KYC verification. Complete your KYC first.
How can I join a live class?
Go to Live Classes, find the upcoming class, and click Join when it goes live. Recordings are available after 2 hours.
